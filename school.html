National High School, Sarat Bose Road, Kolkata

National High School, located on Sarat Bose Road in Kolkata, is an esteemed institution founded by N.R. Iyer. Renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, the school boasts a dedicated team of teachers who foster a nurturing and inspiring learning environment. With a strong emphasis on holistic education, National High School prepares its students to excel both academically and personally, making it a cornerstone of the community.
